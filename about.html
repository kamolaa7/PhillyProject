About Us

What are we?

Welcome to Philly Picks — your go-to guide for the best food, entertainment, and shopping in Philadelphia. Whether you’re craving mouthwatering eats, looking for exciting experiences around the city, or searching for top shopping spots, we make it easy with curated recommendations you can trust. Food: From iconic cheesesteaks to hidden-gem cafés, we highlight the must-try flavors of Philly so you never run out of delicious options. Entertainment: Discover the city's vibrant scene — concerts, museums, festivals, local events, and everything in between. Shopping: Explore the best malls, boutiques, markets, and local small businesses for unique finds and great deals.

Who are we?

This website was created by five Drexel University students — Kamola, Janiyah, Maisha, Jojo, and Ashvi — as a group project for their INFO 151 class. As women working together in a traditionally male-dominated field, we’re proud to bring our creativity, technical skills, and passion for Philadelphia into a platform designed to help others explore the city with ease.
