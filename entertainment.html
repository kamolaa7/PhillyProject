    • Philadelphia Entertainment Attractions

    Philadelphia's Magic Gardens

    1020 South Street, Philadelphia, PA 19147
    A dazzling indoor-outdoor mosaic art environment created by Isaiah Zagar. Wander whimsical tunnels and courtyards filled with tile, glass, and found objects.
    Pricing: Adults $15, Students $12, Children $8

    Philadelphia Museum of Art

    2600 Benjamin Franklin Parkway, Philadelphia, PA 19130
    One of the largest art museums in the U.S., featuring collections from Renaissance to modern works. Explore world-class galleries and climb the famous “Rocky Steps.”
    Pricing: Adults $25, Students $14, Children under 18 free

    Blue Cross RiverRink

    101 S Christopher Columbus Blvd, Philadelphia, PA 19106
    A seasonal waterfront rink offering ice skating in winter and roller skating in summer. Enjoy skating, games, and events along the Delaware River.
    Pricing: Admission $5, Skate rental $12

    Kimmel Center for the Performing Arts

    300 S Broad Street, Philadelphia, PA 19102
    A stunning glass-domed venue on the Avenue of the Arts. Home to the Philadelphia Orchestra and Broadway tours, with concerts, theater, and dance performances.
    Pricing: Varies by performance (typically $30–$100+)
